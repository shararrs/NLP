 Fantom Drives/Micronet VENOM8T NVMe PCIe Gen 4 M.2 Up to 4TB SSD with 176-Layer 3D NAND 
 3,000TBW endurance, 1.6 million hours of MTBF 
 
                                    This is a Press Release edited by StorageNewsletter.com on September 27, 2022 at 2:02 pm                                 
 
 
 Fantom Drives , brand of  Micronet Technology , announced  VENOM8T  performance portfolio of NVMe SSDs for throughput gaming, virtual reality, 4K/6K video production, and business applications.  
 
 These drive models are available in 1, 2 and 4TB capacities and feature easy installation with Gen 4, PCIe, Gen3, Gen2, and Gen1 motherboards. 
 The global NVMe market size is expected to reach $353.78 Billion at a steady CAGR of 29.5% through 2028, according to latest analysis by  Emergen Research . “ Growth of NVMe market can be attributed to the rising demand for SSD among various consumers across the globe as it supports low latency and operational performance in differing applications .” 
 The  TVENOM8  SSDs  line  with PCIe Gen4 x 4 interface ranks as  one  of the fastest flash storage drives available in the consumer market, with industry  RW  speeds. In addition to the above listed motherboard compatibilities, VENOM8 models also offer plug-and-play compatibility with PlayStation 5 gaming consoles. Using the Micron 3D TLC NAND technology, th is  line provides video/audio professionals, gamers and business professionals with performance solution possible to run transaction-intensive software and games. 
 Features of VENOM8 drives include: 
 
 
 RW performance speeds up to 45x faster than HDDs, 13x faster than SATA SSDs, and 2x faster than previous generation M.2 NVMe drives. 
 
 
 Simple installation and compatibility with Gen4, PCIe, Gen3, Gen2 and Gen 1 motherboards. 
 
 
 Fast PC and console gaming load time performance with gaming library capacity. Load games faster than stock PS5 SSD and store up to 100 directly playable games with the 4TB VENOM8. 
 
 
 Flash storage integrated with 176-layer 3D NAND technology and  SK hynix Inc.  DDR4 DRAM Cache. VENOM8 is equipped with TRIM support, SMART, APST, and LDPC ECC algorithm. 
 
 
 Reliability with 3,000TBW endurance, 1.6 million hours of MTBF, and an extended 5-year warranty. 
 
 
 Military-grade data security with End-to-End Data Path Protection and AES 256-bit encryption. 
 
 
 Available VENOM8 models include: 
 
 
 VM8X10 : 1TB Drive – UPC: 749656173378 
 
 
 VM8X20 : 2TB Drive – UPC: 749656173385 
 
 
 VM8X40 : 4TB Drive – UPC: 749656173392 
 
 
 “ VENOM8 drives provide very performant SSD storage for customers seeking a better gaming, virtual reality, digital video production, or business application experience, ” said Hamid Khorsandi, CEO, Fantom Drives. “ With zero supply chain delays for immediate availability, our VENOM8 line of drives have shown incredible read/write speeds in testing, making these drives ideal for throughput-intensive consumer applications.” 
 You can find the product at retailers, including  Walmart.com ,  Newegg.com ,  BHPhotoVideo.com , or  Amazon.com . Products can also be purchased on the company’s  website .
